Corgi

Welsh Pembroke Corgi is a cattle herding dog breed that originated in Pembrokeshire, Wales. The corgi is one of rhe smallest herding dogs. Pembroke Welsh Corgis are famous as the preffered breed of Queen Elizabeth II. Pembroke Welsh Corgis are very affectionate, love to be involved in the family, and tend to follow wherever their owners go. They look like foxes.
